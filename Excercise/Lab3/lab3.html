fruitweb pest and disease models

For effective regulation of pests and diseases with minimal use of pesticides current location-based information is the most important requirement. This applies both to the orhganic, as well as to the integrated management. Modern simulation software in combination with local weather reports and weather forecasts offer the opportunity to simulate the situation of pests and diseases in the orchard. The fruit grower receives an important decision support for the planning of crop protection. A more effective use of pesticides is possible.

Currently, the following forecast models and calculation tools in fruitweb are offered:

  • fruitweb scab model (primary ascospore season)
  • fruitweb secondary scab (leaf- and fruit scab)
  • fruitweb Cydia (codling moth)
  • Calculation of temperature sums
